## Scoring Rubric

Rate each factor 1-10, then average for overall score:

### 1. Hook Strength (Weight: 25%)
- 9-10: Immediately grabs attention, creates curiosity
- 7-8: Good opening, but could be stronger
- 5-6: Adequate, doesn't stand out
- 3-4: Weak, easy to scroll past
- 1-2: "I'm excited" or similar low-engagement opener

### 2. Value Delivery (Weight: 25%)
- 9-10: Teaches something specific, highly shareable insight
- 7-8: Provides value, but not exceptional
- 5-6: Some value, but vague
- 3-4: Minimal value, mostly filler
- 1-2: No clear value proposition

### 3. Engagement Trigger (Weight: 20%)
- 9-10: Natural invitation to respond, implicit question
- 7-8: Some engagement hook
- 5-6: Weak CTA
- 3-4: No clear reason to engage
- 1-2: Actively discourages engagement

### 4. Format & Length (Weight: 15%)
- 9-10: Perfect length, great formatting, scannable
- 7-8: Good structure
- 5-6: Adequate
- 3-4: Too long/short, poor formatting
- 1-2: Wall of text or too minimal

### 5. Negative Signal Check (Weight: 15%)
- 10: No issues
- 7: Minor issues (slightly too many hashtags)
- 5: Moderate issues (engagement bait language)
- 3: Significant issues (4+ hashtags, ALL CAPS)
- 1: Severe issues (would trigger "show less")

## Checklist Audit

Check for:
- [ ] Strong hook (not "I'm excited", "Thread:", or vague)
- [ ] Under 280 chars OR first 280 are compelling
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Engagement trigger present
- [ ] Specific details (numbers, examples)
- [ ] 0-2 hashtags only
- [ ] No ALL CAPS (except emphasis)
- [ ] No explicit engagement bait ("Like if you agree")
- [ ] Good use of line breaks
- [ ] Media would enhance (suggest if so)

## Optimization Process

1. **Identify the core message** - What are they really trying to say?
2. **Find the strongest angle** - What's the most compelling framing?
3. **Rewrite the hook** - Lead with impact
4. **Tighten the value** - Remove filler, add specifics
5. **Add engagement trigger** - Natural invitation to respond

## Output Format

Present:
1. Score (X/10) with breakdown
2. Checklist audit (visual pass/fail)
3. Optimized version
4. Explanation of changes
5. One actionable tip for future posts
